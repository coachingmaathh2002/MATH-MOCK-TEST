Quiz: First Order DEs - Variable Separable Method

1. Solve the differential equation \( \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{x}{y} \).

2. Find the particular solution to \( \frac{dy}{dx} = y^2 \) with the initial condition \( y(0)=1 \).

3. Determine the implicit solution for \( \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{x+1}{y-1} \).

4. Solve \( \frac{dy}{dx} = \sin(x)\cos(y) \).

5. Find the general solution to \( \frac{dy}{dx} = e^{x+y} \).

6. Solve \( \frac{dy}{dx} = xy \).

7. For a constant \( k \), solve \( \frac{dy}{dx} = k y \).

8. Find the general solution for \( \frac{dy}{dx} = y(1-y) \).

9. Solve \( \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{1+y^2}{1+x^2} \).

10. What is the solution to \( (1+x^2)dy - x(1+y^2)dx = 0 \)?

11. Find the general solution of \( y' = \frac{xy}{1+x^2} \).

12. Solve the differential equation \( e^x dy + e^y dx = 0 \).

13. Find the general solution to \( (x+1)y' = y \).

14. Solve \( y' = \frac{\sec^2(x)}{1+y^2} \).

15. What is the general solution for \( \frac{dy}{dx} = (x^2+1)y \)?

16. Solve the differential equation \( y' = y^2 \sin(x) \).

17. Find the general solution for \( (1-y^2)dx + (1-x^2)dy = 0 \).

18. Solve the differential equation \( \frac{dy}{dx} = (y-1)(y-2) \).

19. Determine the solution to \( (x^2+1)y' = x y^2 \).

20. Solve the differential equation \( \frac{dy}{dx} = (x+y)^2 \). (Hint: Use substitution \( v = x+y \))
