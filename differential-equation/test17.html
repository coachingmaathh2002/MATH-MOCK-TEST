Ordinary Differential Equations Quiz

Test your knowledge on the order, degree, and formulation of ODEs.

1. What is the order of the differential equation \( \frac{d^3y}{dx^3} + 5x \left(\frac{dy}{dx}\right)^2 - 7y = \sin(x) \)?

2. Determine the degree of the differential equation \( \left(\frac{d^2y}{dx^2}\right)^3 + \left(\frac{dy}{dx}\right)^5 - y = 0 \).

3. What is the order of the differential equation \( \left(\frac{d^2y}{dx^2}\right)^{3/2} = x + \frac{dy}{dx} \)?

4. Find the degree of the differential equation \( \left(\frac{d^2y}{dx^2}\right)^{3/2} = x + \frac{dy}{dx} \).

5. Consider the equation \( \frac{d^2y}{dx^2} + e^{\frac{dy}{dx}} = x^2 \). What are its order and degree, respectively?

6. Which of the following differential equations represents a first-order, first-degree ODE?

7. The rate of change of temperature of a body is proportional to the difference between its temperature and the ambient temperature. If \( T \) is the body's temperature and \( T_a \) is the ambient temperature, which ODE represents this statement?

8. What is the order of the differential equation \( \frac{d}{dx} \left( y \frac{d^2y}{dx^2} \right) + x \left(\frac{dy}{dx}\right)^3 = 0 \)?

9. The population of a certain species grows at a rate proportional to its current size. If \( P(t) \) is the population at time \( t \), which ODE models this?

10. What is the degree of the differential equation \( y''' - (y'')^{1/2} + y' = 0 \)?

11. Formulate the ODE for a mass \(m\) attached to a spring with spring constant \(k\), undergoing simple harmonic motion without damping. Let \(x(t)\) be the displacement from equilibrium.

12. What is the degree of the differential equation \( \left(\frac{dy}{dx}\right)^2 + \frac{d^2y}{dx^2} = \cos(x) \)?

13. Identify the order of the differential equation \( \left(\frac{d^2y}{dx^2}\right)^2 + y \frac{dy}{dx} + x = 0 \).

14. A tank contains 1000 liters of water with 10 kg of salt. Brine containing 0.01 kg/L of salt flows in at 5 L/min. The mixture flows out at the same rate. Let \(Q(t)\) be the amount of salt in the tank at time \(t\). Formulate the ODE for \(Q(t)\).

15. What is the order of the differential equation \( \sin\left(\frac{d^2y}{dx^2}\right) + \frac{dy}{dx} = x \)?

16. What is the degree of the differential equation \( \sin\left(\frac{d^2y}{dx^2}\right) + \frac{dy}{dx} = x \)?

17. A certain radioactive substance decays at a rate proportional to the amount present. If \( N(t) \) is the amount of substance at time \( t \), which ODE models this decay?

18. What is the degree of the differential equation \( \frac{d^4y}{dx^4} + \left(\frac{d^2y}{dx^2}\right)^2 + y = 0 \)?

19. Formulate an ODE for a falling object with air resistance proportional to its velocity. Let \(v(t)\) be the velocity, \(m\) its mass, and \(g\) acceleration due to gravity.

20. What is the order and degree of the differential equation \( \ln\left(\frac{dy}{dx}\right) = xy \)?
